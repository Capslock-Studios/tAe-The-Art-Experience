This peice is my perspective of what the 3rd eye can see. Why it sees it and what we can do with it

  • by Siya Percy
  • timeless read
  • 29 Comment

Lately I've been seeing a repeation in human behaviour and while I experience this, my mind tells me that I am special for learnig this but then my gut argues agaist it. It argues that if I see it, then everyone else see it too and many have taken their place in the repeatition. It is a must that one takes a place in the repeatition...That! or opening the 3rd eye. Opening the third eye is abstract...it is more than just thinking things, more like living in a state of your own imagination while clearly intune with the collective reality, the global conscience. the logic from the human experience...the rest of the world. It is undertanding what the bigger picture requires of you while everything that matters to you is your little reality. This, you will fight for. You will not let anyone question your judgement...

As you read this, you will wonder how true is this...it doesn't matter!!

What matters is the task at hand. What ever project you're working on right now...That's your little picture. Even if it will affect the whole world at some point...right now it is your little picture... See it...See you.... Open ALL your eyes... Wider, wider, Take a deep breath, Hold it, Hold it, Breath... Look at the painting, Find yourself....

You want to be where the spots are on black and White. If you are on the vibrating spots you are distracted. If you are on the maze, you need to meditate... If you gravitate to wards the two red spots, you are blind and that is danger.

Before writing about this I had to take some time to search the properties and it's metadata to find out at precisly what time I made the sketch...

A concept that enforces itself.

On the 7th of July 2023 at 5:03 on my way from a meditation session at MOAD I walked into the where I was "chilling" with my friends and right after entering I felt like I should have someone loking over my back even though I am in a room with people that I think are watching my back.

It is at this moment that I realised that one humanbeing must always have two views in their head but I was with a crowd so I couldn't write the thought to make sense nor would I have the time nor tools to properly convery the experience. So I quickly grabbed my laptop and squiggled this picture...

The big A and the small A. I also added a path onf the smaller A becuase I could see something happening but I could make sene of it. Many days went past and I kept on seeing this picture on my laptop and I keep remembering that it is important but I can't see it's importance. Until today August 24 22:02:23

I had a lot on my mind and while in thought my mind drifted and I found myself imagining the younder and innocent me Then the now me and then it hit me...

I am not the only one who has a not on his mind, almost everyone that I interacted with today is fighting a small battle...in a small picture and we all wanna win.

So today without even looking at the first image I started imagining it...picuring how I probably drew it, the started drawing shapes on the wall using my finger. It went from Big, small, big small to Big, Big, small, small... Then the balls started movinga and as they say, the rest is hitory.
