The New Kids!!!

There's a certain mood or feeling that we would like the person reading this to feel, that "peace"..."It's okay" you can do it and you are not alone. Founded by a mixture of two powerful yet contrasting energies. A logical thinker and the emotionally inteligent, the art experience is a platform developed to incubate up and coming talent while empowering the developed talent to grow farther, reach heigher.

We do this through a series of programs aimed at creating and expanding markets for the creative and innovative. Here's a list of our services:

  • Business to Business Service
    We are a business to business service focused at using our network resources to generate leads for our clients.
  • Auctioning Arts and Other mediums
    We auction out all types or arts and other mediums through our digital platforms and warehouses.
  • Hosting Events
    We host events specifically aimed at marketing the goods and services of our clients in a very artistic and engaging manner.
  • Hosting Competitions
    We host competitions and give out prices to some who need such opportunities

tAe | The Art Experience was founded as the embodiment of the founder's desire to build an African City in the West so as to bridge the gap between western technology and African growth. The aim is to aquire enough resources to build an all African business hub in New York City thus Positioning ourselves at the heigh way of western development. This allows us young businesses to pivot the South African economy through foreign exchange.

We intend on doing this by running a "people first" business model as we understand that some of the people that have the tools we need to developing a brighter future for humainity are yet to be discoverd, even by themselves.
